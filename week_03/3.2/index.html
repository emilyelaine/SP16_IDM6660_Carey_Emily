Types of Diets

The Paleo Diet

Description

A paleo diet is a diet based mainly on foods similar to those supposedly available to early humans in prehistoric times. It is based on what proponents claim paleolithic humans likely would have eaten, such lean meat, fish, fruit, vegetables, root vegetables, eggs, and nuts.

Foods to avoid:

  • Dairy Products
  • Grains
  • Sugar
  • Alcohol
  • Coffee

The Plant-Based Diet

Description

A plant-based diet is a diet of any animal (including humans) based on foods derived from plants, including vegetables, whole grains, legumes and fruits, but with few or no animal products.

Foods to avoid:

  • Meat
  • Eggs
  • Dairy Products
