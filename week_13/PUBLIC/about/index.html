About

Hi. It’s me. Jed, of JedEditedIt Productions. You’re probably thinking, “That name is really difficult to say.” You’re not wrong. I’d change it, but I already bought the domain.

I’m a video producer, voice actor, and musician. I mostly work alongside the GRAMMY-Award winning family music duo, The Okee Dokee Brothers.

I’ve produced 3 feature-length videos, a lot of music videos, and some wedding videos.

While I specialize in the post-production area of video editing, I have experience in many roles of the production process: scriptwriter, director, production sound/boom operator, camera operator, you get it. You could say I wear many hats, but you’d be wrong. My head is too small and hats make me look weird.
